# Sales-analysis-Dashboard
An end-to-end Power BI analytics project exploring BMW’s global sales, profitability, and product positioning. The report is structured into three specialized pages to provide executive, financial, and product-level insights
# Dataset used
<a href="https://github.com/ksreeii/Sales-analysis-Dashboard/blob/main/BMW_Car_analysis%20dashboard.xlsx"> Dataset
# Dashboard pages 
# 1. Sales Overview
- KPIs: Total Sales ($376M), Total Profit ($94M), and Units Sold (15K).
- Regional Performance: Comparative analysis of 5 global regions and 3 sales channels (Dealership, Online, Wholesale).
- Trends: 5-year yearly sales trend analysis showing a peak in 2021 ($77.1M).
# 2. Profit Analysis
- Efficiency: Monitoring a 25% Profit Margin.
- Time Intelligence: Monthly profit tracking against the previous year's performance.
- Geospatial Insights: Profit breakdown by country (USA, Mexico, Canada) and regional color preferences (e.g., White and Black dominating in Africa/Asia).
# 3. Product Insights
- Pricing Strategy: Analysis of the "Top 5 Models by Price," led by the BMW M5 (₹1.99 Cr).
- Tech Specs: Integration of technical data (Engine, Power, Weight) for high-performance models.
- Volume Leaders: Identification of the BMW Z4 as the primary revenue driver ($17.1M).
# Process
- Data transformation:Used Power Query to clean and structure raw automotive datasets, ensuring currency consistency and model categorization.
- Data Modeling: Established a star schema to link sales transactions with regional geography and product technical specifications.
- DAX Development: Created custom measures for Profit Margin %, Previous Year Comparison, and Sales Rankings.
- Implemented a dark-mode theme with intuitive navigation icons to allow seamless switching between Sales, Profit, and Product views.

# insight of 3 pages
# 1. Sales Overview:
- This page serves as the mission control for high-level decision-making.
- Successfully tracked a global revenue of $376M with a $94M total profit.
- Identified Africa ($82M) and South America ($75M) as the leading markets by volume.
- Visualized that the Dealership channel consistently outperforms Online and Wholesale across all five continents.
- Captured a significant sales surge in 2021 ($77.1M), providing a baseline for future forecasting.
# 2. Profit Analysis:
This page focuses on the "why" behind the numbers, focusing on margins and seasonality.
- Maintained a healthy 25% Profit Margin across the product line.
- Pinpointed August ($8.6M) as the peak profit month, while using "Previous Year" overlays to identify a growth slowdown in July.
- Used a Treemap to reveal that White and Black models yield the highest profit density in Asia and North America.
- Identified the United States and Mexico as the top individual contributors to the $94M profit pool.
# 3. Product Insight: Inventory & Specs
- This page bridges the gap between sales data and product engineering.
- Highlighted the BMW M5 as the portfolio flagship with a price point of ₹1.99 Cr.
- Linked high-performance specs (e.g., the M5’s 635 HP) to consumer demand and pricing tiers.
- Showcased the BMW Z4 as the "Best Selling Model" ($17.1M), proving it is the primary driver of inventory turnover despite not being the most expensive.
- Categorized the Top 5 models to help sales teams prioritize high-margin luxury vehicles like the 7 Series and X5.
